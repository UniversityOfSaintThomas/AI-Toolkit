About the AI Toolkit

The AI Toolkit is a collaborative initiative at the University of St. Thomas, bringing together faculty, STELAR instructional designers, and university librarians to transform academic work through AI-assisted approaches. Our goal is to gather, feature, and share creative uses of Generative AI in teaching, course design, and research—making this a resource where you can explore ideas to integrate into your own practice and benefit from the experiences of others across our community.

Continue Your AI Journey

Curious about how AI can enhance your teaching, learning, or research? Explore the ideas and resources in our AI Idea Book to find inspiration and practical strategies you can try in your own work. If you’d like additional support, you’re welcome to connect with our team to learn more about the toolkit or discuss ways to incorporate AI into your practice. Reach out through STELAR ( stelar@stthomas.edu ), book an AI Consultation , book a librarian consultation to learn about AI research tools , or discover more on the Institute for AI for the Common Good site.
